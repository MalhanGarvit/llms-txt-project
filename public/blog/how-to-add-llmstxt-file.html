How to Add llms.txt File to Your Website if You're Not a Techie

Published on July 30, 2025 · 4 min read

In today's AI-driven world, controlling how artificial intelligence interacts with your website content has become essential. The llms.txt file is emerging as the standard way to communicate your preferences to AI crawlers and large language models. But what if you're not technically inclined? Let's explore how to implement this important file without diving into code.

What is an llms.txt File?

An llms.txt file works similarly to robots.txt but specifically for AI systems. It allows you to:

  • Define which content AI assistants can access
  • Specify how your content should be attributed
  • Set boundaries for AI training on your content
  • Guide AI tools on how to present your information

Why Your Website Needs an llms.txt File

Adding an llms.txt file provides several benefits:

  1. Control over your content - Determine how AI systems use your website material
  2. Better visibility in AI results - Help AI assistants accurately reference your content
  3. Protection against misrepresentation - Prevent AI systems from mischaracterizing your brand
  4. Improved SEO for AI search - Optimize for the next generation of search technology

For maximum effectiveness:

  • Be specific about permissions and limitations
  • Include clear attribution guidelines
  • Update your file as your content strategy evolves
  • Reference specific sections of your website when applicable
